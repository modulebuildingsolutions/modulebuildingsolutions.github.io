Why Modular Building Solutions?


We expect to be able to add at least 30% to your gross development margin from savings in build costs and funding costs. Your capital is therefore tied up for a much shorter period – this gives you the ability to take on many more opportunities. We simplify the process. We are a one stop shop. We take care of design, consenting, site preparation, module installation and finishing.

We have unlimited factory capacity and an exclusive in-house team of NZ based importation experts and offshore based quality inspectors The modules arrive finished - even including the furniture if required


How do we do it?


The earlier you involve Modular Building Solutions the better – ideally modular units are designed in early concept design. Existing designs can be adapted any time. A change to modular can be made after siteworks has been done right up until building commences.

Even allowing for the extra time for re-design, re-consenting and shipping, the end date can still be achieved as the manufacturing time is so much shorter than site construction times (see timeframe comparison)

The completed modules are insured, securable assets when received by our freight forwarders in China. NZ funders should release payment based on this
