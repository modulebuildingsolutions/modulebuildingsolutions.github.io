Who's It For?


Property Developers


We are focused on Property Developers. These could be private developers, community & social housing providers, retirement village or rest-home developers. Modular Building Solutions units are also suitable for small property developers. We’d like to talk to any home-owner who has a full site with potential for terrace housing..

There are many more development opportunities now with the Auckland Unitary plan and proposed plan changes in Tauranga and other centres. We will start with an evaluation of your site for development potential, provide design options, manage the subdivision and building consent process.

We aim to build long-term, meaningful partnerships with property developers to deliver multiple successful projects over time. This means we are committed to fulfilling what we promise!
