MODULAR BUILDING SYSTEMS PROCESS


The earlier you involve Modular Building Solutions the better – ideally modular units are designed in early concept design.

  • We understand that for your current projects you are committed to the specification, unit size, layout, look and feel. We will therefore match this in a modular alternative. We will retain any NZ specified products that are non-negotiable.

  • A change to modular may be able to be made as late as the scheduled start of planned building works.

  • Even allowing for the extra time for re-design, re-consenting and shipping, the end date can still be achieved as the manufacturing time is so much shorter than site construction times

  • The completed modules are insured, securable assets when received by our freight forwarders in China. NZ funders release payment based on this
