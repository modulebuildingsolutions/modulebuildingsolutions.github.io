FAQ

Isn’t the quality of Chinese products inferior to NZ products?

This is true of some Chinese products. However, we are only interested in supplying quality products that meet or exceed the NZ Building Code (50 year + life) or equivalent international standards. We aim to eliminate this perception.

Can I get spare parts for Chinese products in NZ?

Yes, we will import spare parts and replacement products so that these are available if required.

Aren’t you taking jobs away from New Zealanders?

NZ currently has a housing crisis and cannot build houses quickly enough. Houses are also unaffordable for an increasing number of people, especially young people. It’s true that most of the on-site building work is substituted by factory manufacturing in China, however, by providing houses quicker and at a better price point, we are enabling people the opportunity to purchase their own home that wouldn’t otherwise be able to. This provides a net benefit to NZ.

Can I buy a single house from Modular Building Solutions?

Yes, however this would either need to be designed from scratch or adapted to enable consistency of module sizes
